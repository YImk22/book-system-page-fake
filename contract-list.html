Contracts Module Description: Manage contracts for books held by authors or agents, including agent name, author, advance payment, royalty rates, royalty report recipient, contract start/expiry dates, and complimentary copies address. Supports tiered royalty rate structures based on book version (hardcover/paperback) and sales volume, with each contract linking to corresponding book information, sales data, and profit analysis.
